\doxysubsubsubsubsubsection{Подробное описание}
A "{}main"{} or "{}root"{} graph defines a namespace for a collection of graph objects (subgraphs, nodes, edges) and their attributes. Objects may be named by unique strings or by integer IDs.

\doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_ga2ca7f6ee15e2ed41b86b715d776ab230}{agsubg} finds or creates a subgraph by name.

\doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_ga1c19df4ad0b6221455f56230f7ef3252}{agidsubg} allows a programmer to specify the subgraph by a unique integer ID.

A new subgraph is initially empty and is of the same kind as its parent. Nested subgraph trees may be created. A subgraph\textquotesingle{}s name is only interpreted relative to its parent.

A program can scan subgraphs under a given graph using \doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_gabcd8466586e7f9dafd183b291427231f}{agfstsubg} and \doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_gae997729f968b4ecc622d1a5eedf2b785}{agnxtsubg}.

A subgraph is deleted with \doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_gab1c83c6ec6158127af8d718352cea710}{agdelsubg} (or \doxylink{group__cgraph__graph_ga3ee45d1d24d5cd3752fa361aea60d17e}{agclose}).

The \doxylink{group__cgraph__subgraph_gaf7bfbe45c1682b483146507823af9007}{agparent} function returns the immediate parent graph of a subgraph, or itself if the graph is already a root graph.
