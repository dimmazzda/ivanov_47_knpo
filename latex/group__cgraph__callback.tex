\doxysubsubsubsubsubsection{Подробное описание}
An \doxylink{group__cgraph__callback_ga1268b501cf6ea9359343e0545e0d89bc}{Agcbdisc\+\_\+t} defines callbacks to be invoked by Libcgraph when initializing, modifying, or finalizing graph objects. Disciplines are kept on a stack. Libcgraph automatically calls the methods on the stack, top-\/down. Callbacks are installed with \doxylink{group__cgraph__callback_ga536796e152d4e2880c6754897dacb1ca}{agpushdisc}, uninstalled with \doxylink{group__cgraph__callback_ga04dc55e336444df2689c70304e91a769}{agpopdisc}.
